Silicon Valley AI Summit 2025

April 20, 2025
9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
San Francisco Convention Center
Organized by AI Research Foundation

Join industry leaders for a day of insights into the latest AI advancements and their impact on business and society. This premier event brings together researchers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts to explore cutting-edge developments in artificial intelligence.

About This Event

The Silicon Valley AI Summit 2025 is the premier gathering for AI professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts. This full-day conference will feature keynote presentations, panel discussions, and interactive workshops covering the latest advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Topics will include:

  • Generative AI and its applications across industries
  • Ethical considerations in AI development
  • AI for sustainability and climate solutions
  • The future of human-AI collaboration
  • Advances in natural language processing and computer vision

Schedule

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Registration and Networking Breakfast

10:00 AM - 11:30 AM: Opening Keynote: "The Next Frontier in AI"

11:45 AM - 1:00 PM: Panel Discussion: "AI Ethics and Governance"

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Lunch Break and Networking

2:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Breakout Sessions (Multiple Tracks)

3:45 PM - 5:00 PM: Industry Applications Showcase

5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Closing Keynote and Networking Reception

Who Should Attend

This event is designed for AI researchers, data scientists, software engineers, product managers, entrepreneurs, investors, and anyone interested in the future of artificial intelligence and its impact on business and society.
